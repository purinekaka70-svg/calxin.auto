Our Story

Founded in 2020, Calxin Auto Mombasa has focused on quality spare parts, honest sourcing, and direct customer support across Mombasa and beyond. What started as a small parts outlet has grown into a trusted local supply point for auto components and support.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and integrity has made us a dependable destination for engines, braking parts, electrical components, accessories, and other automotive solutions.
