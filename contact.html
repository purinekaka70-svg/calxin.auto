Frequently Asked Questions

How do I confirm price and payment?

The website only collects your request. Final price, stock confirmation, and payment method are agreed directly with Calxin Auto after live chat or WhatsApp confirmation.

Do you deliver spare parts?

Yes. Delivery and pickup options are confirmed after the owner reviews your request and checks stock availability.

How do I know the part is available?

Send the part through the request list, live chat, or WhatsApp. The admin team then confirms stock, condition, and the best current price before you commit.

What are your business hours?

Our listed support hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday to Sunday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How can I speak to the owner quickly?

For urgent stock checks or direct follow-up, call 0706 931802 or open WhatsApp from the website. Live chat is also available after sign in.
